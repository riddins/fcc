Izzy

This is Izzy. She's the sweetest of cats, but she can have an attitude as well.

  • She's fluffy and fuzzy
  • She likes to play...when she likes to play
  • She protects her space when she wants her space for herself

Izzy was a rescue. My wife was introduced to her as a kitten by a vet who found her in a woodpile.
